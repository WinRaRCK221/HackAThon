2021 Stevenson Hackathon

A project about sustaining energy use through production

Made by Nandan Harish, Rohith Komati, and Ashwin Vazhayil

Overview:

This game is about clicking the energy icon and getting as much energy through the use if the solar panel. However, every 5 seconds there is a 20 percent chance that the energy will be solit in half. So be careful where your next upgrade is becuase you don't want it to be less than the cutoff or it's game over.
